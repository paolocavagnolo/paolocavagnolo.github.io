Paolo Cavagnolo is a maker, freelance, based in Torino.
He studied nuclear engineer and after the bachelor's degree he began a career as an entrepreneur opening a social company with some friends. He helped the making of TechLab, the makerspace of Chieri that he currently manages and helps developing.
Paolo helps the local community understand the evolution of technology: organizing workshop and lectures, and enabling accessibility to tools, desk and machines, both analog and digitals. He also work in understanding how technology could help people with disability in different fields, especially with music.
